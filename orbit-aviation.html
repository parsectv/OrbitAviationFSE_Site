About the Group

Serious simulation.
Real community.

Orbit Aviation was founded on the belief that virtual aviation should mirror the precision, professionalism, and camaraderie of real-world airline operations — all within the rich FS Economy ecosystem.

01

Professional Standards

We hold our members to realistic operational procedures, from pre-flight planning to cargo management and economy tracking across the FSE network.

02

Collaborative Operations

Group flights, shared aircraft leasing, and coordinated hub-and-spoke operations mean no pilot flies alone. Build real connections through a shared virtual economy.

03

Progressive Advancement

Earn rank and recognition through logged hours, cargo missions, and group contributions. A clear path from First Officer to Captain to Fleet Command.

How to Join

Ready to join
Orbit Aviation?

We welcome pilots of all experience levels. The only requirements are a working FS Economy account, a compatible flight simulator, and a commitment to professional operations.

01

Create an FSE Account

Register at fseconomy.net and familiarise yourself with the simulation network and cargo mechanics.

02

Join Our Discord

Connect with the team, ask questions, and get a feel for our community before committing to apply.

03

Submit an Application

Fill out a short application form including your FSE pilot ID, simulator platform, and available flying hours.

04

Complete Orientation

A brief check-in flight with a senior member confirms you're set up correctly. Then you're cleared for operations.
