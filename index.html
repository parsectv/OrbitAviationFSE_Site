About the Club

Fly with purpose.
Not just for fun.

Orbit Aviation was founded on the idea that virtual aviation is worth doing properly. We operate within FS Economy — a persistent simulation economy where every flight matters, every cargo run counts, and every decision has consequences.

01

Professional Standards

Realistic operational procedures from pre-flight to cargo delivery. Every flight is logged and contributes to the group's shared economy.

02

Collaborative Operations

Shared aircraft, coordinated hubs, and group missions mean you're never flying alone. Build something real alongside pilots who care.

03

Structured Advancement

Earn rank through logged hours, cargo missions, and contributions. Climb from Second Officer to Fleet Command — on merit.

How to Join

Ready to fly the line?

Open to all sim pilots. You need an FS Economy account, a compatible sim, and a willingness to fly with standards.

01

Get on FSE

Create an account at fseconomy.net and get familiar with the cargo and economy mechanics.

02

Join Discord

Drop into our server, introduce yourself, and get a feel for the crew before applying.

03

Submit Application

Short form — FSE pilot ID, your sim platform, and how many hours a week you expect to fly.

04

Fly Orientation

One check-in flight with a senior member. Prove you're set up and ready. Then you're cleared.
